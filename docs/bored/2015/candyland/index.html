A Low Carb Candyland

Apr 18, 2015 

Candyland, a game where a group of children take a convoluted path through a diabetes filled utopia, needs a revision. No child should be exposed to such a gluttonous journey at such an early age. On the other hand, hipsterfying this into Vegatableland is not an alternative. It's widely known that the day the Cookie Monster selected a carrot over a cookie, over ten thousands angels cried in unison. I believe a happy medium would, at least, involve a quick closed-loop tour of Candyland that does NOT end at King Candy's suspicious looking castle. (We, as a society, should not support people who lure children into candy filled houses)
