As a huge fan of logic puzzles and retro gaming, I decided to merge those two and take a shot at building a Game Boy Advance. The result is a collection featuring: Nonogram, Path Loop, Light Up and Binario. Every puzzle is procedurally generated, and I’ve carefully filtered out any that can’t be solved through step-by-step forward logic. That means you’ll never have to guess - pure deduction is all you need.
