Summary: In this post, I'll share two of my favorite examples of elegant game design.

YINSH and Can't Stop are two of my favorite board games.

YINSH is an abstract strategy game where players slide rings around the board, flipping markers as they move to create rows of five. Can't Stop is a push-your-luck dice game where you roll to advance up columns, risking your progress each time you choose to keep going. Both games are easy to learn, have minimal rules and offer endless replayability.
